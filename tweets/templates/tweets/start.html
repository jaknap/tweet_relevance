About

This application classifies the Twitter trends to pre-defined categories. The trends/hashtags will be more meaningful because you will know what category it belongs to. The tweets shown under a trend are again filtered to show tweets by the database of users maintained by the application. These users are celebrities or popular people with verified twitter accounts.
